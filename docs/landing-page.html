FAQ

What’s the “proof” run?

You get one full pipeline run free: we discover jobs, score them, tailor your resume and cover letters, and let you apply to up to 20. Enough to see it work for you and get real feedback (e.g. callbacks). No credit card. If it works, upgrade to Premium for unlimited runs and auto-apply.

Do you actually submit applications for me?

Yes. On Premium you can turn on auto-apply. We open the application page, fill in your details, upload your tailored resume and cover letter, answer screening questions with AI where needed, and submit. Supported on many job boards and Workday; some sites we flag for manual apply.

I’m employed but want better options. Is this for me?

Yes. Lots of users are employed and want optionality — a better role, raise, or escape hatch. You set your searches and run discovery when you want. No pressure; you control how often you apply.
